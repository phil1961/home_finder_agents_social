The Problem

Real-World Example

What a typical session looks like.

Saturday morning, coffee in hand.

You open HomeFinder and see 8 new listings since yesterday's fetch. Two are in West Ashley — your target area. You click the first one. It scores 71/100 on your preferences. The AI flags that it's been on the market 94 days with two price reductions, and suggests opening at $412,000 against the $449,000 ask. The second listing scores 83. No price changes, newer construction, above flood plain. You star both as favorites.

On your phone, you open the Tour Planner, check both addresses plus two from last week, and tap "Open in Google Maps." By noon you've walked through four houses. That evening you add notes to each — what you liked, what bothered you — and check "Visited." Next morning you hit Portfolio Analysis on your favorites. The AI tells you the 94-day listing is your best negotiating opportunity, and the 83-scorer is the one to move on quickly.
