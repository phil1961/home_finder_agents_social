What is HomeFinder?

HomeFinder aggregates listings from Zillow and Realtor.com, scores every property against your personal criteria using 17 weighted factors, and provides AI-powered deal analysis. Unlike listing sites that serve both buyers and sellers, HomeFinder works exclusively for the buyer — including negotiation advice, suggested opening offers, and honest assessments no listing portal would ever give you.

Understanding Deal Scores

Each listing is scored 0–100. The composite is a weighted average of 17 factor scores.

82 75+ = Great deal
63 50–74 = Decent match
34 Below 50 = Weak match

Notes, Visit Tracking & Tour Planner
My Notes (Detail Page)

Every listing has a private notes section — up to 3,000 characters. Notes autosave when you click away. Check off your visit status:

  • Scheduled a Visit
  • Visited
  • Not Interested After Visit
  • Made an Offer
Tour Planner

Available from the Tour nav link (logged-in users). Shows all favorites and maybes with their visit status.

  1. Check the houses you want to visit today
  2. Enter your starting address (optional)
  3. Tap Open Route in Google Maps
  4. Navigate with all stops pre-loaded
Settings
Help Level

Guided — tooltips + inline help. Standard — tooltips on hover. Expert — no hints. Change it above or on the Settings page.

Power Mode

Controls which nav items and scoring sliders are visible. Low = essentials. Mid = more controls. High = everything.

AI Mode

Off — hides all AI buttons. On — standard analysis. Tune — personalized analysis with your Buyer Profile.

Tips
  • Use the Map view to find clusters of listings near your target areas.
  • Sort by Deal Score descending to see your best matches first.
  • Use Export CSV on the Digest page to download a spreadsheet of your filtered listings.
  • Hidden listings stay in the database — filter for them to recover anything you hid by mistake.
  • Run the AI Analyst before calling your agent about a listing — the negotiation section gives you a prepared position.
  • On driving days, pre-check your tour stops in the Tour Planner the night before.
  • Fill out your Buyer Profile in Settings for dramatically better AI analysis.
  • Lower the Great Deal threshold on Preferences if you're not seeing any green badges on the dashboard.
