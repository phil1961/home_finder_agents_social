AI Deal Analysis

Claude AI scores every listing on 17 factors, writes negotiation briefs, and ranks your favorites side by side.

Every listing gets a composite deal score (0-100) based on price, size, yard, features, flood risk, HOA, taxes, and more. You set the weights -- care more about yard size than price? Slide it up. The AI then writes a plain-English brief:
  • Single listing: negotiation strategy, suggested offer range, red flags
  • Portfolio: compare all your favorites, find patterns, pick the dark horse
  • Preference coaching: AI reviews your scoring weights and suggests adjustments
No account needed to see scores. Free account unlocks AI analysis.
Share With Anyone

Send listings to friends and family with a personal note. They can react, comment, and start their own search.

Click the share button on any listing card or detail page. Add a recipient email, pick a relationship (friend, family, coworker, neighbor), and write a personal message.
  • Recipients get an email with the listing photo, price, and your note
  • They can react: Love It, Interested, Great Location, Too Pricey, Not For Me
  • You see all reactions on your "My Shares" page
  • Or just copy a shareable link -- no email required
Guests can share too -- no account needed.
Add a Home

Know a home for sale that isn't on Zillow? Submit it as a Neighborhood Tip. Agents review and score it.

Under Social > Neighborhood Tips > Add a Home, fill in what you know:
  • Address, zip, price, beds/baths/sqft, photos (up to 5), and a description
  • Pick the relationship: your home, friend, neighbor, or family
  • Confirm you have permission to share the listing
Your submission appears as a "Neighborhood Tip" with a green badge. If you have an assigned agent, they see it in their dashboard and can enrich it with details (year built, lot size, features, lat/lng), then approve it. Approved tips become real scored listings alongside Zillow and Realtor data. Submissions expire after 90 days if not approved. You earn 5 points for each submission.
Earn Points

Share listings, invite friends, add homes, get reactions. Earn points and climb the monthly leaderboard.

  • +1 -- Share a listing with someone
  • +2 -- Create a collection
  • +3 -- Someone reacts to your share
  • +3 -- Agent approves a community listing
  • +5 -- Submit a Neighborhood Tip
  • +5 -- Agent creates a listing directly
  • +10 -- Your referral signs up
Daily cap: 50 points. Check your balance in the Social dropdown (gem icon). View full history at Social > My Points. Top earners appear on the monthly Leaderboard.
Build Collections

Group your top picks into shareable collections. Perfect for comparing options with your partner or family.

Create a collection from Social > My Collections. Give it a title and description, then add listings from any listing card or detail page using the "Add to Collection" dropdown.
  • Share an entire collection with one email -- recipients see all listings at once
  • Make collections public for anyone with the link, or keep them private
  • Reorder listings by position to tell a story (e.g., "Best to Worst")
  • Earn 2 points for each collection you create
Plan Tours

Flag favorites, plot them on a map, and build a driving itinerary. Your agent sees everything you pick.

Star listings you want to visit. The Tour page plots all your favorites on an interactive map and builds a driving route.
  • Add notes and mark listings as visited, scheduled, or "made offer"
  • If you have an agent, they see your flagged listings and notes in real time
  • Export your favorites as CSV from the Digest page for offline use
Street Watch

Watch a street you love. Get emailed the moment a new listing, price drop, or status change appears.

On any listing detail page, click "Watch This Street." Enter your email (or sign in) and you'll get notified whenever something changes on that street:
  • New listing appears matching the street name and zip
  • Price drops on an existing listing
  • Status changes (e.g., pending, sold, back on market)
Manage all your watches from the Watch page. Unsubscribe anytime via email link. Works for guests too -- no account needed.
Social Proof

See which listings are trending. "Most Shared This Week" and share counts show you what others love.

The dashboard surfaces community activity so you can spot trends:
  • Most Shared This Week: horizontal scroll row of the top 5 listings people are sharing
  • Share count badges: each listing card shows how many times it's been shared
  • Reaction summary: on the detail page, see grouped reactions (Love It x3, Interested x5, etc.)
  • Monthly Leaderboard: top 10 sharers and top 10 referrers for the current month
