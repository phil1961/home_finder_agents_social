How It Works

Up and running in four steps.

01

Choose your market

Select the city or region below. HomeFinder fetches live listings from Zillow and Realtor.com for your area every night.

02

Set your priorities

Use the Preferences page to weight what matters: price, yard, flood risk, school proximity, single story — whatever your situation calls for.

03

Browse scored listings

Every active listing gets a composite score tailored to your weights. Sort, filter, star favorites, and take notes on any property.

04

Get AI deal advice

Open any listing and request a Deal Brief — strengths, concerns, negotiation strategy, and a suggested opening offer, generated in seconds.
